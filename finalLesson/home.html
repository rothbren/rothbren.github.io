Welcome

Are you looking to explore the beautiful lands of Cozumel? Or looking to try something new? If you said yes to both those questions, we have the perfect thing for you! Welcome to Scoots, the scooter rental company here to help you find your perfect adventure. We offer a variety of tours and sizes of scooters for your convience. It doesn't matter if you are a party of 20 or a party of one, come join us and let's go on an adventure.

Rentals

You don't need to buy or bring your own scooter, we provide them for you! We offer a variety of prices and rental options. From Honda Moter Scooters to ATV Side-by-Sides, we will find the perfect scooter for you. Be sure to check out our rentals page before booking a reservation.
